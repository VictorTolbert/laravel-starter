Order Summary Table

The Order Summary table shows you a rolled-up view of the data as defined by your filter criteria (either by advertiser or campaign, or by a date range).

Field Description
Order #

The unique Videa order number.

Click the order to open the Order Detail screen.
Status

The status of the order:

  • Accepted
  • Rejected
  • New
Type

The type of order:

  • Original
  • Revision
AdvertiserOrganization or person who pays for the production, execution, and placement of an advertisement.

The advertiser for this order, based on your traffic data.
AgencyAn organization acting as an agent for an advertiser devoted to developing and placing ads for the advertiser. The agency (advertising agency) represents an advertiser in the process of buying media. The agency typically takes a commission (commonly 15% or less) on the gross cost of each ad. The commission is typically paid as a discount on the invoice from the media partner to the agency. The agency bills the advertiser the gross amount of the invoice.

Agency buying the campaign.
CPE

Client Product Estimate. A code from the buyer that indicates the advertiser, product, and estimate number connected to the order.
Start

Start date of the campaign.
End

End date of the campaign.
Currency

Shows the type of currency used for the order:

  • Cash
  • TradeThe exchange of quantities of a broadcaster's ad time for merchandise or services. See also barter. (BarterCommercial breaks that have already been sold by a syndicator and arrive to the broadcaster ready to air along with program content. Also referred to as barter breaks.)
Revenue

Monetary gain realized by the seller from this campaign.
SharePercentage of households that are tuned to a specific program at a specific time.

Share realized by the campaign. The share is initially calculated using revenue divided by availed market budget, but can be overridden by the station or Rep assigned to the account.
