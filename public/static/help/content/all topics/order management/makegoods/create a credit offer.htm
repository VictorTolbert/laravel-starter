Create a Credit Offer

  1. From the Videa main menu, click Order Management > Order Search. The Order Search screen opens.
  2. Find the order you want to add a credit to.
  3. Under the Manage Schedule column for the order, click Manage.
  4. In the Details Add Offer box, select the Credit option.
  5. Select the line to credit.
  6. Click Add Offer. The Offer Information - Credit screen opens.
  7. Enter any MakegoodAn ad offered by a station in place of a scheduled ad that was pre-empted. Comments to the buyer.
  8. Select the line.
  9. FillThe practice of filling open time on a log before appending to automation. Fill Spots are used for clients, promos, or PSA spots. in the number of spots in the weeks to be credited.
  10. Make sure that all Totals are correct.
  11. Select a Classification for the makegood.

  12. Do one of these:

    • Click Save to create a draft copy.
    • Click Send to send the offer to the buyer.

  13. Do you need to search the station avails to add a spot?

    • If yes, follow these instructions:
      1. In the Station AvailsAvailabilities. Unsold spots available for sellers to sell to buyers or advertisers. Also refers to a seller's submission of program and rating estimates for advertising planning and buying. section, enter the search criteria as described in Search Station Avails Field Descriptions.
      2. Click Search. A listing of available spots populates the table. The table lists all the available avails with the currently approved rates and ratings for the search criteria you selected.
      3. Select the line where you want to make the offer.
      4. Select the Days you do not want to offer.

        The default is to select all days. You can remove days from the offer by unchecking the day in the list.

      5. Enter the number of spots for each date you want to offer in the calendar.
      6. Enter the SpotThe television time bought market-by-market by an advertiser who wants to promote their company or brand. RateThe price set for an individual piece of inventory. This price is generally a function of the demand for the inventory combined with its expected audience delivery. for the offer.
      7. Enter the Station IMP (impressions) for the offer.
      8. Enter any Makegood Comments you want to add.
      9. In the Makegoods Spots Offered section, click Add Spots. The spot offer moves to the Makegoods Spots Offered section.
    • If no, go to the next step.
  14. At the top of the screen, click Save to save the makegood.
  15. When the makegood is ready, click Transfer to MediaOps. The Videa Media Operations team will send the makegood to the buyer and rep for verification.

    The Transfer to MediaOps button is not available until you save the makegood.
