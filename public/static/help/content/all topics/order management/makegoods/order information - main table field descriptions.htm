AgencyAn organization acting as an agent for an advertiser devoted to developing and placing ads for the advertiser. The agency (advertising agency) represents an advertiser in the process of buying media. The agency typically takes a commission (commonly 15% or less) on the gross cost of each ad. The commission is typically paid as a discount on the invoice from the media partner to the agency. The agency bills the advertiser the gross amount of the invoice. InvoiceAn electronic document that lists in detail the date, time, length, and rate of an ad insertion. It may be delivered in the SpotData/Donovan Invoice format.
