Field Description
Order Count

The number of spots in the order.
AdvertiserOrganization or person who pays for the production, execution, and placement of an advertisement.

The advertiser for this order, based on your traffic data.
AgencyAn organization acting as an agent for an advertiser devoted to developing and placing ads for the advertiser. The agency (advertising agency) represents an advertiser in the process of buying media. The agency typically takes a commission (commonly 15% or less) on the gross cost of each ad. The commission is typically paid as a discount on the invoice from the media partner to the agency. The agency bills the advertiser the gross amount of the invoice.

Agency that booked the campaign or order.
Revenue

Monetary gain realized by the seller through the Videa system for this advertiser this broadcast calendar year.
SpotThe television time bought market-by-market by an advertiser who wants to promote their company or brand. Volume

Total number of spots purchased through the Videa system.

Station Order #

Order number of the order at the station.
CPE

Client Product Estimate. A code from the buyer that indicates the advertiser, product, and estimate number connected to the order.
FlightA scheduling tactic that alternates between periods of advertising with periods of no activity. The flight is a subset of an order’s overall start and end dates. For example, you place an order that starts on October 1st and ends on October 28th. Within that order, there are defined flights of October 1 to October 7 and then again on October 15 to October 28, skipping the week of October 8. The process of creating these patterns is called flighting. Start

Start date of the campaign.
Flight End

End date of the campaign.
Revenue

Revenue realized by the order.
Market BudgetBuyer: The dollar amount to be spent in each market. Seller: The amount of money a broadcaster is expected to bill within a month, quarter, or year.

Market budget of the campaign.
Demo

Default demographic of the campaign.
SharePercentage of households that are tuned to a specific program at a specific time.

Share realized by the campaign.
