AgencyAn organization acting as an agent for an advertiser devoted to developing and placing ads for the advertiser. The agency (advertising agency) represents an advertiser in the process of buying media. The agency typically takes a commission (commonly 15% or less) on the gross cost of each ad. The commission is typically paid as a discount on the invoice from the media partner to the agency. The agency bills the advertiser the gross amount of the invoice.

FlightA scheduling tactic that alternates between periods of advertising with periods of no activity. The flight is a subset of an order’s overall start and end dates. For example, you place an order that starts on October 1st and ends on October 28th. Within that order, there are defined flights of October 1 to October 7 and then again on October 15 to October 28, skipping the week of October 8. The process of creating these patterns is called flighting. Start Date
