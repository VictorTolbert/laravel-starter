How Does Videa Work?

We automate the buying process, not the deal. The station makes its full inventory available for purchase through Videa. Buyers can access automated, full schedule, forward reserve, local inventory is delivered in seconds.

We normalize the traffic, optimize pricing for that inventory, and then facilitate the purchase.

What is "Normalized" Traffic?

We understand it’s common for broadcast groups to have different traffic systems, and historical data can be difficult to analyze since it requires significant cleaning and can span multiple source systems, or it can be difficult to access after a system upgrade.

Videa’s technology collects, transforms, and processes up to four years of historical traffic data – regardless whether multiple traffic systems exist across station groups.

  • We securely pull in four years of historical traffic data using TVBTelevision Bureau of advertising. and BXF standards to facilitate usage.
  • We then “smooth” the data by removing noise, seasonality, specials, and anything out of the ordinary the station ran to find a pattern, allowing us to see the TV viewer numbers with more granularity.
  • Once the pattern is established, seasonality is added back in and fed into the Videa InventoryCommercial spots available on a station. Also known as avails. Management System
